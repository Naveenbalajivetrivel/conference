Call for papers

Novel research and/or review papers on the state-of-the-art and visionary research contributions that present new innovative perspectives, ideas, conceptual and empirical contributions with potential impact in the future are invited to this conference.

Particularly invite novel contributions that are based on (but not limited to) the following topics as they pertain to intelligent data processing techniques, intelligent control, and optimisation of smart and dynamic systems.
