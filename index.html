international conference on Research Methodologies in Knowledge management, Artificial intelligence and telecommunication engineering (IC-RMDMATE'23)

01-02, November 2023 | Chennai, India

Scope

With the continuous increase in data availability, the computing and storage capacity of Information and Communication Technology [ICT] systems are rapidly expanding, and the technological innovations such as Artificial Intelligence [AI], Big Data and intelligent control are beginning to have a huge impact on different research and engineering domains. Ranging from smart industries to healthcare, these technologies have the potential to alter many aspects of our society. However, numerous obstacles remain, ranging from dealing with data quality and size limitations, which are restricting the widespread adoption of scalable and robust solutions. This conference on Research Methodologies In Knowledge Management, Artificial Intlligence And Telecommunication Engineering (IC-RMDMATE'23) invites novel and unpublished research contributions that address the existing challenges and/or showcase the state-of-the-art real-world applications and enabling innovative technological advancements of the latest technology.

The main aim of IC-RMDMATE'23 is to present the state-of-the-art research innovations and results in the area of intelligent systems and advanced control technologies and real-world applications in information and Communication research, particularly covering autonomous systems, artificial intelligence, sensing, hardware and software implementation, soft computing, hardware/software-oriented optimization, intelligent control design, intelligent system integration and control applications, Quantum Computing, Big Data and other relevant research areas. IC-RMDMATE'23 intends to bring together researchers from both academia and industries, who are working on emerging topics of artificial intelligence and control techniques.
